Dog Bakery

Bow Wow Beauty Shoppe will be setting up their famous Dog Bakery Bar to provide guests of the canine variety with delicious, nutritious, and decadent delectables! Everything from cakes, truffles, bon-bons, and cookies will be available.

Dog Bakery products are all natural, organic, and pet safe so you can rest assured that they will be enjoying top quality treats at New Orleans Nights. For those interested in learning the craft, try your hand at decorating your own dog treats to take home for Fido. Additionally, each furry guest is guaranteed 3 treats with their ticket but feel free to build a to-go box for a reduced price.

BONUS: Bow Wow Beauty Shoppe will also be offering some of their favorite dog apparel for purchase on site as well as minor dog grooming services. Nothing like a new tuxedo and a good brushing to make a pup feel pampered and ready for the Ball! Check out their website for all your pre-Fur Ball needs!
