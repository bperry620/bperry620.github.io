Doggy Daycare

While the Fur Ball is a pet-friendly event, sometimes you just want to let loose on the dance floor without a leash wrapping round your ankles! For those moments you want to mingle with the two-legged guests without interruption we have top-notch pet sitting available. Liberty Station offers ample grassy fields for frolicking, fetching, and friend-making which guarantees your pet a great time. Our play area is safely enclosed and carefully attended and fear not--our daycare is on location so you will always be within sight of your best friend.

Petco has generously donated a variety of toys to our daycare facilities and beds are available should naps be needed! A fresh water supply and clean-up amenities are provided.Still have questions? Check out our Frequently Asked Questions page and don't hesitate to call or email us at Furball@sdhumane.org
