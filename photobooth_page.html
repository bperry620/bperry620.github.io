Oh My Dog! Photobooth

Our friends at Oh My Dog! Photography have arranged to be on site capturing the special moments sure to arise at Fur Ball:New Orleans Nights. As an added bonus, they will be setting up their pet-friendly photobooth for commemorative photos! Be sure to stop by for shots of your furry friend or get in on the action yourself. Props and costume options will be available on site for both you and your pet including bow-ties, top hats, parasols, and everything a southern Bell or Gent may need for a night on the town in the French Quarter! Photos will be posted on this page after the event for you to download and share.

For those of you fluent in the filters of Instagram, use #SDHSFurBall2018 the night of the Ball to be automatically entered into our Southern Charm Photo Contest. The photo with the most likes will win a dinner for two and beer tasting at Stone Brewing in Liberty Station! For more information on Oh My Dog! Photography and to check out some of their great work just click the button below and explore their website.
