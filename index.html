EVENT INFO

The San Diego Humane Society is pleased to announce the theme of the 2018 Fur Ball Gala: New Orleans Nights! Please join us Saturday, October 6, 2018 at Stone Brewing in Liberty Station for an open-air evening of cajun-inspired cooking, New Orleans Jazz, and plenty of raffle and auction opportunities. (including an all-inclusive trip to the Big Easy!)

While you dance the night away to the swinging songs of The California Honeydrops your furry friend will enjoy homemade treats courtesty of Bow Wow Beauty Shoppe and plently of opportunities to play. Oh My Dog! Photography will be on site with a pet friendly photobooth for capturing the special event and Bud's Louisiana Cafe will be sure to keep you full and satisfied with a smorgasbord of sweet, savory, and spicy NoLa classics.
