Firefly Contest

The SDHS Fur Ball is our biggest annual fundraiser filled with ample opportunities to donate and give back to the San Diego community. But what would a good party be without prizes and games? With that, we are excited to announce the addition of the Firefly Contest to this years Fur Ball--all atendees are welcome and ecouraged to participate as three lucky winners will walk home with three grand prizes!

Hidden throughout the Fur Ball venue are three golden fireflies (see photo), the first three guests to find these glowing glitterbugs and turn them in to a SDHS team member will win a dinner for two at Mister A's. Winners under 17 get a family four pack of tickets to Legoland. While fireflies are easily spotted in the evening hours, don't be fooled into thinking they will be quick to discover. Our SDHS fireflies can be hiding in the most unexpected of places so keep you eyes peeled while you take a turn around the dance floor or sample the dessert table!
