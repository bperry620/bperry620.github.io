Bud's Louisiana Cafe

Widely considered some of the best Cajun and Creole-style cooking in San Diego, Bud's Louisiana Cafe will be catering a thorough and filling multi-course meal at New Orleans Nights. From seafood gumbo and hearty jambalaya to classic red beans & rice and pecan pie. Bud's will ensure you're well-fed with nothing but the greatest soul food this side of the Mississippi. For what goes better with a star-lit night and New Orleans jazz than a hearty meal and friends to share it with!

While Bud's will be catering the entire event, a hand crafted and personalized meal by Louisiana Cafe's chef John Smith is available under our Sponsorship Opportunities. This experience allows for an up close and well curated meal from appetizer to dessert and is perfectly paired with wine and beers from Stone Brewery. For more information on Bud's Louisiana Cafe, head over to their website and explore their menu!
